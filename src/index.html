What I am currently working on

Currently I spend my time at work thinking about the architecture of our ServiceNow platform, including adding features to improve productivity as well as creating new value for our internal and external customers. In addition to that I am trying to bring to life CSDM (Common Service Data Model) in our business and apply it best to our very tricky specifics of a private provider of managed services.

I am also a solution architect for PATHFINDER which is a business continuity solution for the health and care sector. Its main goal is to help organizations keep essential services operational during system shocks and unpredictable events (e.g. pandemic, heatwave, or staff shortage).

In personal life I spend a lot of time learning new things, practicing and improving my French, and trying to do more DIY things (especially self-hosting and development).
